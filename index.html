2017 - 2020 BSc -"Solving SAT problems using quantum algorithms" Faculty of Mathematics, Informatics, and Mechanics, Poland Warsaw

In this work, we analyze 2-SAT and MAX-2-SAT computational problems in the context of quantum computations. The computations were carried using the D-Wave quantum computer as well as two quantum simulators SimCim and Wildqat. In this thesis, we formulate 2-SAT and MAX-2-SAT problems as well as necessary concepts from quantum computing. We describe the Ising model and express the MAX-2-SAT problem in this model. We also prove that the 2-SAT problem is solvable in polynomial time.
